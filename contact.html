Contact & Collaboration

One window for all collaboration.

During the initial phase, all inquiries (campus, industry, government, and international) are handled by the center's administrative window. Collaboration inquiries are accepted from August 2026.

Inquiry

How to submit a collaboration inquiry.

Email the administrative window with the following information, and the center will arrange a needs interview before matching you with suitable research teams:

  • Name
  • Affiliation / unit
  • Email
  • Collaboration type (see list)
  • Brief summary of your needs
